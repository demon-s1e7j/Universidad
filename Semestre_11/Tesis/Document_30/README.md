# IPZ Thesis Template

Authors: Sophia Schlosser and Laurence Brandenberger 
Last update: December 2025 (version 1.0)

Purpose: This template provides students of the Department of Political Science at the University of Zurich with a full thesis outline, including setup and structural building blocks. 

The use of the thesis template is to be discussed by the student with their supervisor to accommodate custom features the supervisor wishes to add/change to the template.

# Template Usage 

We advise students not to play around with the margins in the style-file as they have led to downstream complications in our testing phase.

We also advise students to use 12 or 11 points font size, to ensure captions and footnotes are not too small (and to ensure the UZH font does not break).
