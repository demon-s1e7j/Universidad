\unnumberedchapter{Declaration of the Use of AI Tools}

%This is an example text for the declaration of the use of AI tools. 

Generative artificial intelligence, specifically ChatGPT 4o and DeepL Write, was utilized to translate, refine and enhance the clarity, coherence and readability of the written text. This included tasks such as rephrasing, improving sentence structure, and ensuring a polished academic tone. The use of AI was not limited to language optimization and was also used to assist with coding. Overall, it did not affect the originality, technical content, or scientific contributions of this thesis. The use of AI tools was discussed and approved by the thesis supervisor, in accordance with the responsible and transparent use of AI in academia. In Table \ref{tab:ai-tools} the employed tools and their scope of use are summarized.
